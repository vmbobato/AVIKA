Find Your Information

Ready to pay? Need help? Contact us

Where to find your Invoice Number

  • On your statement: Bottom-right in the “Invoice Details” box.
  • By phone: Call us — we’ll verify your identity and provide it.

Where to find your Account Number

  • On your invoice: Look near “Patient Account #” (often mid-page under the payment box).
  • By phone: We can confirm after identity verification.

Where to find your Practice Name

  • Statement header (top of the page).
  • Appointment reminders or visit summaries.
  • Clinic signage/website from your last visit.
If you saw more than one provider, use the clinic/department name printed on your statement.

Use your Legal Name

Enter your name exactly as it appears on your statement (first, middle initial if listed, last). If a parent/guardian is the account holder, use the account holder’s name.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can’t find my Account Number?

Email billing@avika-bs.com or call (281) 653-2924. We’ll verify your identity and help.

What if my name changed?

Use the name printed on your latest statement or call us to update your account.

Practice Name is unclear

Use the clinic name on your statement. If you visited multiple locations, choose the one tied to the specific invoice.
